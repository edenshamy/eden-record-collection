Indigo

~ RM ~

“Indigo is the debut studio album by South Korean rapper RM of BTS, released on December 2, 2022, through Big Hit Music. The album marks the rapper's first full-length body of work since Mono and serves as a documentation or archive of his late twenties.” – Wikipedia. Ayesha and I listened to this vinyl for the first time together, and I actually became an unexpected fan. The opening track has remarkably chill vibes. I also like “Still Life,” and “Wild Flower.”
