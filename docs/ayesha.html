Ayesha's Collection

Ayesha is my roommate and one of my closest friends. She is also a junior, but she's a pre-med student majoring in mathematics (like a real psychopath). Ayesha is a very talented artist, hard-working student, and one of the kindest people I've ever met. Sophomore year, she joined a K-Pop dance group called K-Beat. Now, she's the Vice President!

Ayesha fell in love with k-pop at age 10, and I don't think she'll ever fall out of love. My lengthy knowledge about the genre comes entirely from her tangents about BTS, Ateez, P1 Harmony, and a bunch of other groups I cannot keep track of! Let's just say, she is extremely hyped for the upcoming reunion tour of BTS.

Although Ayesha collects tons of K-Pop merchandise, photo cards, and albums, only a few of them come in the form of vinyl. She mostly purchases CDs, which often come with photo-albums, stickers, and other surprises featuring the "idols" in the group.
