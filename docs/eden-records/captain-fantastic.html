Captain Fantastic and the Brown Dirt Cowboy

~ Elton John ~

“Captain Fantastic and the Brown Dirt Cowboy is the ninth studio album by English musician Elton John, released on 23 May 1975 by DJM Records in the UK and MCA Records in the US.” – Wikipedia. I adore Elton John, and especially this album cover. This is a lovely vinyl that I enjoy putting on while I cook or just lounge around. My favorite song off this one is the title track!
