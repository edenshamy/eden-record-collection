Goodbye Yellow Brick Road

~ Elton John ~

“Goodbye Yellow Brick Road is the seventh studio album by English singer, pianist, and composer Elton John. A double album, it was released on 5 October 1973, by DJM Records.” – Wikipedia. This record is full of classic Elton hits like “Candle in the Wind,” “Benny and the Jets,” and “Saturday Night’s Alright for Fighting.” Who doesn’t love to sing and dance along to some Elton John?
