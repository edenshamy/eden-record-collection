Metallica (Black Album)

~ Metallica ~

“Metallica is the fifth studio album by American heavy metal band Metallica. It was released on August 12, 1991, by Elektra Records.” – Wikipedia. I became obsessed with Metallica in middle school when I dove headlong into classic rock and metal. The Black Album is full of some of their biggest hits and my favorite songs, like “Enter Sandman, “Wherever I May Roam,” “Sad But True,” and “The Unforgiven.”
