A Record of Records

Planet Waves

~ Bob Dylan ~

“Planet Waves is the fourteenth studio album by American singer-songwriter Bob Dylan, released on January 17, 1974, by Asylum Records in the United States and Island Records in the United Kingdom.” – Wikipedia. My freshman year roommate in the Kinsolving Dormitories gifted me this vinyl. I love Bob Dylan, and this is a funky album with a boisterously gritty sound that especially grew on me after a second spin.
